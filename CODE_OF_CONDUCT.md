# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in Demon Tech Roadmap a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Be supportive of other contributors.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on collaboration and learning.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or insults.
- Trolling or disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers through GitHub Discussions or Discord.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant and adapted for Demon Tech Roadmap.
